DIY Recirculating Bird Bath

Supplies needed:

  • 10-gallon food-grade bucket with lid
  • Small recirculating pump
  • Optional:
    • Small rocks
    • Paint

Instructions:

  1. Install the pump:
    1. Using a drill bit with the same diameter as the pump tubing, drill a hole in the center of the lid
    2. Place the pump in the bottom of the bucket
    3. Attach one end of the tubing to the pump
    4. Feed the other end of the tubing through the hole in the lid
    5. Cut a small vertical slit at the top of the bucket and feed the electrical cord through
      • Make sure the slit extends slightly below the rim of the lid
  2. Fill the bucket with water
  3. Decorate the bird bath (optional):
    • Scatter some small rocks on the top; this will make the bath more attractive to the birds
    • Paint the outside of the bath as desired. Get creative!
  4. Plug in the pump
  5. Enjoy!

Maintenance:

  • In order to prevent the pump from burning out, be sure to check the water level in the bath regularly and keep it from dropping too low.
  • Clean the bath weekly or as needed
